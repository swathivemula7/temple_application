About The Ramalingeshwara Swamy Temple!

Nestled in the serene village of Ramalingapuram, Tamil Nadu, the Sri Parvathi Samedha Ramalingeshwara Swamy Temple is a 300-year-old sanctuary of devotion and architectural splendor. This ancient temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is renowned for its rich history. Devotees are welcomed to experience its divine ambiance and partake in its vibrant cultural traditions. Join us in preserving and celebrating this timeless spiritual heritage.

The Ramalingeshwara Swamy Temple is surrounded by beauty of nature and a serene pond, offering an ideal place for deep meditation and dhyana. The peaceful ambiance provides a perfect escape from daily stresses, allowing devotees to connect spiritually.
